How to explore

Your data stays in your browser. Here's how to get around.

Reading the chart

  • DragPan across time
  • ScrollZoom in and out
  • Double-clickReset to the full range
  • 1M–AllJump to a quick range
  • ClickA metric on the left to show or hide it
  • Bottom barDrag or resize to scrub through time

Marking phases

  • The Markers button adds one — pick a date & label
  • Each marker starts a phase that runs to the next
  • Tint shades each phase in its colour (toggle per phase)
  • Click a phase to zoom in · click again to zoom out
  • Hover a phase for its weight change, rate & intake
  • Edit a phase's colour or date anytime in the list
